About Us

Nestled in the picturesque hills of Panchgani, Iconic Villa promises an indulgent escape from the everyday hustle. From a private swimming pool to mesmerizing sunset vistas, every detail is curated for your relaxation and adventure. Conveniently located near paragliding sites and surrounded by nature, our villa also offers the warm services of a dedicated caretaker to cater to your culinary and comfort needs.
